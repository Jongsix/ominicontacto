===================================
Omnileads (OML) 1.4.1 release notes
===================================

*February 27, 2020*


This is a "bugfix" release improving the stability and performance of the codebase.


Fixes and improvements
=========================
- Upgraded Wombat Dialer version in installation to latest (20.02.1-271) due to a bug that made Wombat to keep dialing the contact database even when agents were paused.
